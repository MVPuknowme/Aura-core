# No Public Bias Governance

Operator: Michael Vincent Patrick / MVPuknowme

Statement:

> I do not want public bias.

Grounded system interpretation:

Aura-Core public claims, dashboards, documentation, and outreach should avoid public bias. The system should be framed in agnostic, evidence-based, consent-based, and human-autonomy-preserving terms.

Governance rule:

Aura-Core should not publicly favor or discriminate against people based on religion, non-religion, race, ethnicity, nationality, disability, class, gender, sexuality, political belief, medical status, poverty status, housing status, or social position.

Operational requirements:

1. Use evidence-first language for impact claims.
2. Separate spiritual or poetic statements from measured technical claims.
3. Label targets, hypotheses, and verified metrics distinctly.
4. Preserve privacy and consent in all public reporting.
5. Avoid implying control over people, minds, communities, agencies, or institutions.
6. Present the 93% human-impact benchmark as a target or hypothesis unless independently verified.
7. Use plain, inclusive language suitable for public, legal, scientific, and nonprofit audiences.

Boundary:

This governance note does not weaken the operator identity, authorship, or mission of Aura-Core. It protects the project from misinterpretation and keeps public-facing claims credible, inclusive, and reviewable.
