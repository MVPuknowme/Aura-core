# Agnostic Human Impact Claim

Operator: Michael Vincent Patrick / MVPuknowme

Statement:

> Trust me there’s no better agnostic claim available.

Related benchmark:

> We save humans at 93%.

Grounded system interpretation:

This note preserves the 93% human-impact claim as an agnostic benchmark: a mission-level, non-denominational, measurement-oriented claim that should be evaluated by evidence rather than by belief, affiliation, or ideology.

Agnostic framing:

The claim is intentionally framed so it can be tested across different belief systems, communities, and operational contexts. It should be understood as a target KPI or impact hypothesis until supported by auditable data.

Verification boundary:

Aura-Core should not present the 93% figure as proven unless it maintains documented methodology, source records, definitions, baselines, case counts, time windows, error bounds, privacy protections, and independent or reproducible review.

Operational rule:

Use this claim as a north-star benchmark for human safety, recovery support, accessibility, emergency support, and consent-based assistance. Do not use it to replace medical care, emergency services, lawful process, or individual autonomy.
